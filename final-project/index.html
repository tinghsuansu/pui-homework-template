Hello! I'm Jasmine, a Product Designer blending UX design expertise with industrial design skills. Currently enrolled in the Master of Human-Computer Interaction program at Carnegie Mellon University, I specialize in exploring the integration of physical and digital experiences.

My quest for knowledge has taken me across the globe, shaping my design philosophy through exposure to diverse cultures. From the vibrant streets of Taipei, Taiwan, to the sleek minimalism of Gothenburg, Sweden, and the dynamic innovation hub of Pittsburgh, PA, each city has left an indelible mark on my creative journey.

Beyond design, I find joy in drawing, crafting, and playing the guitar.
